Who are We?

Taj Jobs is an electronic job network that is concerned with publishing job advertisements from major and international companies, newspapers and social media platforms for Jordanians and non-Jordanians wishing to work in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan and the Arab Gulf states and government jobs issued by official authorities to link those wishing to employment and job seekers and qualify and train qualified researchers for the market.

How to write a resume and cover letter is an important step in the search for a job. Resumes and cover letters are used by recruiters to form an initial impression about a job seeker.
